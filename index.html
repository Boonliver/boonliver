About Us

Boonliver International Pte. Ltd. is a Singapore-based company specializing in wholesale trade and distribution. Since our establishment in 2015, we have been committed to providing reliable sourcing and efficient delivery of a wide range of products to businesses across the region in famous companies like Seven Eleven, Sheng Siong, Haidilao, Giant, and so many more.

With a strong focus on quality and consistency, we work closely with suppliers and partners to ensure that our customers receive dependable products and timely service. Our operations are built on professionalism, integrity, and a dedication to meeting the evolving needs of the market.

At Boonliver, we take pride in supporting our clients with flexible solutions, whether through product distribution, logistics support, or tailored supply arrangements. Our team is driven by a simple goal: to deliver value and build long-term partnerships through trust and performance.

As we continue to grow, we remain committed to maintaining high standards in everything we do—ensuring that our customers can rely on us every step of the way.
